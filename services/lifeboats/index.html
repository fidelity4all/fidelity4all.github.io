Rehooking & Modifications

IMO has adopted new proposals in order to enhance the safety of Lifeboat Release and Retrieval Systems (LRRS), with the purpose of preventing accidents during the launching of lifeboats.

As per MSC circ. 1392 states that All Lifeboat Release And retrieval system must be compliant. by the first Scheduled dry docking After 1st July 2014 and no later than July 2019. Whatever may be your existing hook system, We have ROBUST solutions ready to take over the existing system.

How we works?
  • Assessment of your system, to give you best possible advice on modification or on retrofitting.
  • Some of the Lifeboat release and retrieval systems are to be only modified to get compliancy status from IMO, whereas some Lifeboat release and retrieval systems has to be completely retrofitted with new ones.
  • Hence the Assessment is very important and plays vital role in saving your money.
  • Once the assessment is completed Our beloved customers can sit back and relax
  • We will do all the hard work For arranging Class and flag approvals.
  • Once all Documentation part is completed actual installation will take only 4-5 days of time for your 2 lifeboats.
